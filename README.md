## ğŸ›ï¸ The Fragile Republic

A hardcore strategy simulation game that lets you experience the complexity, dilemmas, and ethical predicaments of leading a country. As the newly elected president, you must balance conflicting political, economic, military, diplomatic, and social pressures over an 8-year term.

### Core Gameplay
*   **Handle Crises:** Deal with random political, economic, and military events.
*   **Make Tough Decisions:** Every choice has a probability of success or failure.
*   **Enact Decrees:** Proactively influence national policy.
*   **Manage Your Cabinet:** Appoint advisors who affect your nation's stats.
*   **Balance Key Metrics:** Monitor support, funds, military loyalty, and more.

### How to Run Locally
1.  `npm install`
2.  `npm run dev`
